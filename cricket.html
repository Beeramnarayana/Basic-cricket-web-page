ICC EVENTS

The Cricket World Cup, officially known as ICC Men's Cricket World Cup,[4] is the international championship of One Day International (ODI) cricket. The event is organised by the sport's governing
 body, the International Cricket Council (ICC), every four years, with preliminary qualification rounds leading up to a finals tournament. The tournament  is one of the world's most viewed  
sporting events and is considered the "flagship event of the international cricket calendar" by the ICC.[5]
